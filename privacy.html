Effective Date: 23 October 2025
Last Updated: 28 November 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AstroDev collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you interact with our website and services.

  • Usage Data: interactions with our site, pages visited, time spent, and navigation paths.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage services, respond to inquiries and support requests.

4. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols including SSL encryption, secure servers, and access controls to safeguard your data.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and correction of your personal data.
  • Request deletion or restriction of your data where applicable.
