How long does a Minecraft server setup take?

+

A basic single server setup typically takes 3-5 business days. A full network setup with multiple servers and automated billing takes 1 to 2 months. Timeline depends on complexity and customization requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We only accept bank transfers via iPay.

What's your revision policy?

+

All projects include 1 free revision. Additional revisions are allowed for up to 3 days after delivery. After this period, each additional revision costs $8.

Do you provide support after project completion?

+

Yes! All projects include ongoing support and maintenance. For servers, we offer 24/7 monitoring and quick-response technical support. Maintenance plans start at $20/month.

No, we currently do not offer custom plugin development.
